# HotYogaPower.com — Image-generation prompts

Copy-paste prompts for ChatGPT (GPT-4o image / DALL·E 3) to create on-brand image
assets. Each prompt is self-contained. For maximum consistency, paste the **House
Style** block first, then the asset prompt — or, when generating several, tell ChatGPT
"same style as the previous image."

Grounded in the HYP Yoga vision (reverent, book-like, the *Hot Yoga Power* ↔ *Haṭha
Yoga Pradīpikā* duality, the sun / moon / compass companions, the path to Rāja Yoga)
and the SparkUI aesthetic (Liquid Glass surfaces, the Spark-family colors, the Temporal
Lens tints, sacred geometry).

> **Three rules that make these work**
> 1. **No text in images.** Image models mangle letters; the site overlays all type in CSS. Every prompt says "no text."
> 2. **Cream or transparent background.** Ask for a plain cream `#faf8f5` background (blends into the site) or, if you'll composite, "isolated on a transparent background."
> 3. **Generate 3–4 variants, pick one, then vectorize the gold line-art ones** (any SVG tracer) for crisp scaling + tiny files. Export the rest as PNG at 2×–3×.

## Brand DNA (the cheat-sheet the prompts draw on)

- **Feeling:** opening a beautifully made contemplative book — never a tech/SaaS look. Reverent, calm, spacious, gallery-quality.
- **Palette:** cream paper `#faf8f5`, deeper cream `#f0ece6`, warm ink `#2c2a26`, **dark gold `#b8860b`**, soft gold `#d4a843`, gold tint `#f5edd8`.
- **Spark-family accents (use sparingly, as jewel tints):** Sparkmarks gold `#d97706`, Reflect/TapasAI/journal blue `#1e88b4`, Illuminate/AI-Sparks violet `#7c5cbf`, DharmaGPT sage `#5a8a5e`, SwamiGPT warm brown `#c47a1a`, Voice coral `#e06666`, Listen teal `#009688`.
- **Temporal Lens tints:** Smṛti = amber (past), Sthiti = blue (present), Saṅkalpa = sage (future).
- **Light:** soft, warm, diffused — golden-hour, like a single lamp at dawn.
- **Motifs:** yantra, mandala, **flower of life**, lotus, palm-leaf manuscript, fine Devanagari-inspired linework, compass.
- **Material:** **Liquid Glass** — luminous translucent frosted glass with a bright top-left specular highlight, a soft inner glow, and a thin light rim (the app's signature surface).
- **Avoid everywhere:** text/letters/numbers, neon, corporate flat-blue, harsh reflections, glossy plastic, stock-photo clichés, busy clutter, depictions of specific real gurus or deities.

## Production notes
- **Transparent vs cream:** ask for "isolated on a transparent background" when you'll composite (emblems, pearls); otherwise cream `#faf8f5` blends straight in.
- **Vectorize the line-art** (assets 1, 4–7) for razor-sharp scaling and tiny files; keep 3, 8, 9 as optimized PNG/WebP.
- **OG (asset 2):** generate text-free, then composite the "HYP Yoga" wordmark with `scripts/make-og.py` (swap the flat background for the generated art).
- **Consistency:** the companion trio (4–6) and pillar trio (7) should each be generated in one go, or with "same style as the previous image," so line weight and glow match.
- These are AI-generated and free to use on the site; no third-party stock licensing.
