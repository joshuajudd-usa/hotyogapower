The texts, free

Every HYP verse and Yoga Sūtra in Sanskrit, IAST transliteration, and English. No account required, ever.

Marks that matter

Sparkmarks, Sparklights, and Karma Sparks — plus QuickTerms and the full 172-term glossary.

Two guides

SwamiGPT for the texts, DharmaGPT for the mat. Ten messages a day, free — meet them →

Listen, share, belong

Text and term audio, the Sangha social journal, Share, Export, and 15 typefaces chosen for legibility.

Smart Journal

Daily Verse Reflection, Where to Begin? prompts, and a journal that remembers what you've written.

Reflect with TapasAI

A persistent companion that reads your reflections and grows with you. Reflection Seed surfaces the themes you keep returning to.

Richer entries

Hero Images and linked audio on your reflections — plus unlimited SwamiGPT and DharmaGPT.

Illuminate

Scholarship, illuminated

Comes with Teacher · $10/mo or $110/yr · in the iOS app

AI Sparks

Source-grounded annotations on verses, sūtras, glossary terms, and your own entries — drawing from 22 wisdom traditions, always traceable to a citation.

Temporal Lenses

Smṛti, Sthiti, Saṅkalpa — memory, steadiness, and resolve. Three perspectives on a single reflection: past, present, and what's ahead.

Voice Sparks & TapasAI Enhanced

Twenty-second audio reflections with AI transcription, and TapasAI running on the flagship reasoning model.
