The companions

Meet the three

Ancient Wisdom Keeper

SwamiGPT स्वामी

Rooted in the classical texts — the Haṭha Yoga Pradīpikā, the Yoga Sūtras, the Upaniṣads, and the Bhagavad Gītā. SwamiGPT teaches in the guru–śiṣya lineage: citing verses precisely, breaking down Sanskrit, naming commentators, and tracing connections across the canon.

What does this verse mean? Break down this Sanskrit term What does Vyāsa's Bhāṣya say?

Free with a Starter account · 10 messages/day shared with DharmaGPT · unlimited with Aspirant.

Modern Yoga Teacher

DharmaGPT धर्म

Bridges classical wisdom and contemporary practice. DharmaGPT starts with the mat — āsana sequencing, prāṇāyāma, alignment, nutrition, sādhanā — then connects what you're doing back to the texts. Warm, encouraging, scholarly without gate-keeping. It meets you where you are.

Free with a Starter account · 10 messages/day shared with SwamiGPT · unlimited with Aspirant.

Journal Companion

TapasAI तपस्

A persistent journal companion that reads your reflections, Sparkmarks, and practice patterns — and reflects your own words back with added wisdom. Like talking to a wiser, future version of yourself. Every conversation builds on the last.
