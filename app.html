In the app

What you'll find

AI Companions

SwamiGPT for the texts and Sanskrit, DharmaGPT for living practice, and TapasAI — a persistent journal companion that grows with you.

Smart Journal

A journal that learns you. Daily Verse Reflection, gentle prompts for when you don't know where to begin, and reflections TapasAI actually remembers.

AI Sparks

Illumination, traced to its source. Annotations surface connections across 22 wisdom traditions — and every one is accountable to a real passage.

The Sparks family

Mark what moves you. Sparkmarks, Sparklights, Karma Sparks, Voice Sparks — a quiet record of how you move through the texts.

Listen

Hear the texts. Audio recitations of verses and terms, plus playback of your AI Companions in their own voices.

Built for every reader

Fifteen typefaces chosen for legibility, full Dynamic Type, and a calm reading surface you can set to Sun or Moon.
